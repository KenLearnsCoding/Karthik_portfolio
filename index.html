Who am I?

Hey, I am Karthik Krishnakumar, a designer who specialises in UI Design, UX Design and Graphic Design. At the incredibly old age of 19, I believe I can bring a fresh perspective to a design team or project. I was born in Kerala, India and spent a good half of my childhood in Lagos, Nigeria and now have found myself pursuing my career in the city of Toronto. As someone who has spent half their life being annoyed at minor inconveniences, I decided to centre my life around making designs that not only look good but also alleviate these annoyances from other people's lives.

When I am not designing I am probably channeling my inner Lionel Messi on the football field (I've had trouble calling it soccer). Either way as I said earlier, I am someone who can "bring your visions into reality". If its user friendly, accessible UI designs or eye catching visuals and graphics; I am your guy. Lets collaborate and create!
